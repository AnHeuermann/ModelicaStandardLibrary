cp_Tlow_der

Compute derivative of specific heat capacity at constant pressure, low T region

Syntax

cp_der = cp_Tlow_der(data, T, dT)

Inputs (3)

data

Type: DataRecord

Description: Ideal gas data
T

Type: Temperature (K)

Description: Temperature
dT

Type: Real (K/s)

Description: Temperature derivative

Outputs (1)

cp_der

Type: Real (J/(kg·K·s))

Description: Derivative of specific heat capacity
